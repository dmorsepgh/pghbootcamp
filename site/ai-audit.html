The Process

Five steps. One roadmap.
Built around your business.

This isn't a checklist. It's a structured process that maps exactly where AI fits into how your business actually runs — and where the biggest opportunities are hiding.

1

Pre-Audit Business Questionnaire

Before we ever sit down together, you complete a focused intake form. Revenue model, team size, current tools, biggest time drains, and where you feel most stuck. This isn't busywork — it's the foundation that makes the live session twice as productive.

2

90-Minute Live Deep-Dive Session

One-on-one with your auditor. We walk through your business from the inside out — sales process, operations, customer communication, fulfillment, marketing, admin. Every function. Every bottleneck. Everything that costs you time or money right now.

3

Workflow Mapping & Gap Analysis

We diagram exactly how work moves through your business today. Then we lay AI opportunities on top of that map — identifying the gaps where automation, AI-assisted tasks, or smarter systems would have the highest impact on your bottom line.

4

Prioritized Implementation Roadmap

Not everything can happen at once. We stack-rank your opportunities by ROI and effort — quick wins you can implement next week, medium-term builds for the next 90 days, and longer-term systems worth investing in. You leave with a sequenced plan, not a wish list.

5

30-Day Follow-Up Check-In

One month after the Boot Camp, we reconnect. What's working? What got stuck? What questions came up in implementation? This call keeps the roadmap alive and makes sure you're not back to guessing by week three.
