Pittsburgh SWAT Team

Make outsiders feel
like insiders.

SWAT stands for one thing: Pittsburgh locals who go out of their way to make out-of-towners feel at home. Not a job. A privilege. One you earn — and one you can lose.

🎯

The Mission

Out-of-towners flew in. They don't know the city, the people, or what to expect. The SWAT Team's job is to change that — fast. Restaurant recommendations, transportation, introductions, conversation. Whatever it takes to make someone feel like they belong here.

⚡

The Standard

SWAT members don't wait to be asked. They see someone standing alone — they walk over. They see confusion — they fix it. They see an opportunity to make someone's experience better — they take it. Every time. Without exception.

🏅

The Privilege

SWAT status is earned, not assigned. Platinum members who consistently demonstrate the standard earn the right to wear the badge. It signals something: this person is the reason people come back to Pittsburgh.

🚫

The Line

SWAT status can be removed. If a member stops showing up for out-of-towners — or worse, makes someone feel unwelcome — the badge goes. No appeals. The standard only works if it means something.

How to Become SWAT

You don't apply. You demonstrate.

Platinum members who show up for out-of-towners — at the event, in the community, in the city — earn the invitation. Carter & Drake make the call. The community sees it happen.

Day 2 Panel

The questions you
actually want answered.

Carter & Drake moderate the panel. Here are the questions that will get asked — no softballs, no vague advice, no "it depends" non-answers.

  1. What was the moment you realized AI was changing how you work — for real?
  2. What's the most overrated AI tool right now, and what actually works?
  3. Walk us through a real project where AI saved you serious time or money.
  4. What do most people get wrong when they start automating their business?
  5. If you were starting today with no budget and no team, what would you build first?
  6. What's the one thing people in this room should do the week after this event?
