# Pittsburgh Boot Camp — Attendee Leave-Behind Kit
## What Every Person Walks Out With

---

## THE TRUTH ABOUT EVENTS

People forget 80% of what they hear within 48 hours.

They don't forget what they hold.

A folder. A card. A shirt. A machine. These are memory anchors. Every time they open that folder or fire up that Mac mini, they're back in the room. The event lives longer than the event.

---

## IN-PERSON ATTENDEE KIT (everything in one bag)

### 1. The Mac mini M4
**The big one.** Already in the program. They came for the knowledge — they leave with the hardware to act on it. Every time they boot it up, they think of Pittsburgh Boot Camp. That's not swag. That's a daily reminder.

### 2. The Prompt Cheat Sheet
**One laminated card. Front and back.**

Front:
- The 4 prompts from Demo Day (the exact ones used on stage)
- "The Prompt That Pays" framework — context, objective, tone, constraints
- The content multiplication chain

Back:
- The 90-day implementation checklist (3 priorities per month)
- QR codes: Substack, Platinum sign-up, Boot Camp recordings vault

**Why laminated:** They don't throw it away. It lives on the desk. It gets used.

### 3. The Boot Camp Workbook
**Printed, spiral-bound, ~20 pages.**

- Day 1 session notes template (blank — they fill it in)
- Demo 1–4 build guides (step-by-step so they can replicate at home)
- 90-day sprint planning pages (used live on Day 2)
- "My AI Employee" worksheet — map out the workflow they built
- Space for hot seat notes and panel takeaways

This is the artifact of the event. It captures what happened and gives them a roadmap.

### 4. The T-Shirt
**Pittsburgh Boot Camp · May 2026**

Front: Event logo
Back: "Stop Learning. Start Building."

It's a conversation starter. They wear it at a networking event. Someone asks. That's a referral.

### 5. The Welcome Card (inside the bag, on top)
**A physical card. Handwritten feel, printed clean.**

> Welcome to Pittsburgh Boot Camp.
>
> You're here because you're done watching from the sidelines.
>
> Two days. Real builds. Real results.
>
> Everything you need is in this bag — including the hardware to keep building when you get home.
>
> Let's go.
>
> — Carter & Drake

### 6. The QR Card
**Business card size. Four QR codes.**

- The Implementation Brief (Substack)
- Platinum Plus sign-up
- Boot Camp session recordings (unlocks post-event)
- "Bug Doug" contact (dougbug.com)

Fits in the wallet. Goes home with them.

## THE BAG ITSELF

Not a tote bag. Not a drawstring backpack.

**A simple matte black zipper pouch** — like a travel organizer or tech bag. Something that looks like it belongs in a carry-on. The kind of thing they actually use.

Screen printed or embroidered: Pittsburgh Boot Camp logo on the front. Small. Clean. Not loud.
