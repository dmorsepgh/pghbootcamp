# AI Foundations Platinum Membership – Program Outline

## Positioning

AI Foundations Platinum is the **inner circle** for agency owners, consultants, and operators who want to turn AI and automation into real revenue, not just cool experiments.
It is modeled after classic platinum‑plus style direct‑response groups (Dan Kennedy, Joe Polish, etc.) but focused on modern AI implementation, systems, and deal‑making.

- Regular price: $295/month
- Honorary founding member rate: $195/month (locked in while active)

The core promise:
"Board of advisors + R&D lab for your AI/automation business, with direct access to Carter and Drake and the best ideas from the entire AI Foundations community distilled for you."

---

## Who Platinum Is For

- Agency owners and service providers selling AI, automation, or implementation services
- Operators and in‑house leaders responsible for systems, efficiency, and growth
- Consultants and creators who want to package AI into high‑value offers

Member identity: serious implementers who value speed, leverage, and being early on what's working in AI.

---

## Core Benefits (What They Get)

### 1. High‑Access Coaching With Carter and Drake

- Weekly or bi‑weekly Platinum‑only Zoom calls focused on implementation, offers, and pipeline.
- Guaranteed hot seats where members can bring live problems:
  - Offers and pricing
  - Client projects and delivery
  - Systems and workflows
  - Team and delegation issues

- Direct line via private Skool channel / DM office hours for:
  - Offer and funnel reviews
  - Prompt and workflow audits
  - Loom feedback on builds and client situations

### 2. "Board of Advisors" Structure

- Quarterly 90‑day planning sessions with Carter and Drake to set:
  - Revenue and client targets
  - Core projects and systems to deploy
  - Outreach and deal‑flow priorities

- Structured progress check‑ins:
  - Tracking key metrics (MRR, clients, close rate, hours saved, systems deployed)
  - Accountability on what was committed vs. completed

Platinum members feel like they have a small advisory board, not just a group chat.

### 3. Exclusive Implementation Assets

- Premium templates and systems not available to the general community, such as:
  - AI/automation offer templates and pricing sheets
  - Outreach scripts and follow‑up sequences
  - Client onboarding and reporting frameworks
  - Notion/Sheets/command center dashboards
  - Proven prompt libraries and workflow SOPs

- "Done‑with‑you" implementation labs where these assets are set up live so members walk away with actual working systems, not just theory.

### 4. R&D Lab and Idea Curation

- Drake and Carter actively **admin** the broader AI Foundations community and Platinum, collecting:
  - Member wins
  - Experiments and test results
  - New prompts, agents, automations, and frameworks

- These are distilled into:
  - Weekly or monthly "Platinum Briefings" (short, punchy, high‑signal updates)
  - Loom breakdowns of "what's working now" that members can plug in fast

Members don't have to watch every channel or call; the top 1–2% of insights get pushed to them in a curated format.

### 5. Deal Flow and Opportunities

- Priority for:
  - Implementation partner and white‑label roles when Carter and Drake have overflow
  - Referrals and intros when projects are a better fit for another Platinum member
  - Collaboration on bigger builds and multi‑skill projects

- Warm network of AI‑literate operators, builders, and agency owners who understand the same frameworks and language, making collaboration fast and clean.

### 6. Status and Community Experience

- Platinum badge and role inside Skool, with access to Platinum‑only posts, channels, and calls.
- Small, curated room (cap: 25–50 members) so people feel they're at the "top table," not in a massive public group.
- Public recognition of member wins and milestones in the broader community, positioning Platinum members as leaders and examples.

---

## Delivery Mechanics

### Calls and Cadence

- Weekly/bi‑weekly Platinum call (implementation + hot seats)
- Monthly "What's Working Now" Platinum briefing call or Loom pack
- Quarterly planning sessions (90‑day sprint design)

### Asynchronous Support

- Private Skool channel for Platinum members
- Priority responses from Carter and Drake
- Asynchronous asset reviews (Looms, docs, screenshots) submitted and answered on a schedule

- Publicly positioned as a $295/month Platinum inner circle.
