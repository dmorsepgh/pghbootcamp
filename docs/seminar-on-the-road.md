## WHAT MAKES IT REPEATABLE

The format is locked. The demos are scripted. The kit is designed. The close is rehearsed.

Every city, same bones:
- Day 1: Build Day (5 demos, implementation block, show & tell)
- Day 2: Panel Day (local expert panel, hot seats, 90-day planning, giveaway)
- Evening: City-specific field trip (this is the only thing that changes)

The local field trip is the hook. Pittsburgh gets the Duquesne Incline or a brewery. Nashville gets honky-tonk row. Columbus gets whatever Columbus has. The experience feels local. The product is universal.

## HOW TO SCALE WITHOUT KILLING YOURSELF

### Option A: Run It Yourself
Carter & Drake host every event. High control, high margin, limited by personal bandwidth.
Max: 4–6 cities/year.

### Option B: Licensed Hosts
Train a certified "Boot Camp Host" — someone who runs the format in their city.
- They handle local logistics and filling the room
- Carter & Drake appear virtually for keynote and close (Zoom or pre-recorded)
- Host keeps 60% of ticket revenue, Carter & Drake keep 40% + all Platinum conversions
- Host fee: $2,500 to get licensed + training

10 licensed hosts × 2 events/year × $10,000 host share = $100,000/year in passive revenue

### Option C: Corporate Version
Same format, sold to companies as a private internal event.
- One-day version for corporate teams
- Price: $15,000–$25,000 flat fee
- No ticket sales needed — one check, one client
- Repeatable with the same deck and demos
